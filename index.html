About Us

“Pass Solution” LLC was established in 2018, initially focusing on television programming, documentaries, advertising production, mining equipment, IT services, and foreign trade. Driven by public demand, our scope has since expanded to include technical services and sales of heavy machinery, media and promotion services, technical tools and equipment, and capsule coffee machines and beverages. We prioritize citizen satisfaction, operate with integrity, and are committed to sustainable, long-term operations.

Vision

To be a sector leader and socially responsible company through sustainable, collaborative, and solution-oriented work.

Mission

Promote safety, ethics, and quality while building strong client relationships for long-term stability.

Our Services

Foreign Trade & Online Store

We began our journey in online order trading from the Republic of Korea in 2023. Since then, we've been actively engaged in offering a wide range of Korean products, including beauty items, vitamin supplements, branded clothing, and food. In 2024, we expanded our offerings to include Nescafe Dolce Gusto capsule coffee machines and Starbucks capsule coffee.

Currently, we accept orders through our website Dolcegustomongolia and proudly operate our own physical store in a premium location within the city.

Media Production

Since 2018, we have been delivering professional video production services tailored to the unique needs of clients in media, feature films, and large-scale advertising projects. Our focus is on producing high-quality content that meets the standards of today’s cutting-edge technology.

What sets us apart is our team of passionate and experienced artists, whose expertise and creativity consistently bring ideas to life with impact and precision.

Heavy Machinery & Mining

Since 2018, our company has consistently carried out coal mining, stripping, and transportation operations in full compliance with international standards. We are committed to maintaining the highest levels of quality and efficiency across all aspects of our work.

Spare Parts Store

As part of our expanding operations, we established a heavy machinery spare parts store in 2022. We source and supply essential components for large vehicles directly from trusted factories in China, ensuring reliability, quality, and competitive pricing for our clients.
